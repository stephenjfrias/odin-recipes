Peanut Butter & Jelly Recipe

Descripion

This is a standard peanut butter & jelly recipe.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread of your choice. I'm into this Tuscan-style bread we've started buying from Trader Joe's.
  • Peanut Butter, also your preference. I prefer the crunchy and honey variety
  • Jelly of your choice. I prefer strawberry jam
  • Salt & Vinegar Chips
  • Toaster Oven

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one side of toast.
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  4. Crush some chips on one slice.
  5. Put them together.
  6. Enjoy!
